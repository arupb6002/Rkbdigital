About RKB Digital Academy

Assam's Premier Online Learning Platform

RKB Digital Academy is Assam's premier online learning platform, dedicated to empowering students preparing for government and competitive examinations. Founded by education enthusiast Ruhini Kr Boruah, our mission is to make quality education accessible, affordable, and effective for every learner across the region.

We specialize in comprehensive preparation for Assam Direct Recruitment (ADRE), Assam Police, and other state-level exams through expertly curated MCQs, revision books, and interactive video lectures. Our exclusive "Grade III & IV Revise Book" and high-yield question sets are trusted by thousands of aspirants for last-minute preparation and concept clarity.

Through our strong presence on platforms like Facebook and YouTube, we stay connected with our student community by offering motivational content, exam tips, and the latest updates. RKB Digital Academy is not just an institute — it's a learning movement driven by dedication, discipline, and a passion for success.
