ABOUT RKB DIGITAL

RKB Digital Academy is an Assamese online learning platform focused on competitive exam preparation like ADRE. We offer video lectures, MCQs, and revision materials to support students across Assam

BEHIND RKB DIGITAL

RKB Digital Academy was founded in 2023 by Ruhini Kr. Baruah with a vision to provide quality and accessible education to every student preparing for Assam’s government job exams.
